About Him

Stephen Curry (born March 14, 1988) is a 6'2" American professional basketball player for the Golden State Warriors, widely considered the greatest shooter in NBA history. He is a four-time NBA champion (2015, 2017, 2018, 2022), two-time MVP (2015, 2016), and 2022 Finals MVP. He is known for revolutionizing the game with his three-point shooting.

Stephen Curry has solidified his legacy by leading the Golden State Warriors to four NBA championships in 2015, 2017, 2018, and 2022.
