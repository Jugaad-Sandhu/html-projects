Mickey Mouse

The True Original & Leader of the Club

1. Introduction/Description

Mickey Mouse is a cheerful anthropomorphic mouse who first appeared in "Steamboat Willie" in 1928. He is the official mascot of Disney and is known for his iconic red shorts, large yellow shoes, and white gloves.

2. What do you like about them?

I like Mickey because he is always an optimist. No matter what kind of trouble he gets into with Donald Duck or Goofy, he stays calm and finds a way to fix the problem with a smile.

3. Famous Shows & What I like most

Some of his most famous shows include "The Mickey Mouse Club," "Mickey Mouse Clubhouse," and "Mickey Mouse Mixed-Up Adventures."

What I like most about these shows is how they focus on friendship and teamwork. In "Mickey Mouse Clubhouse," I love the "Hot Dog Dance" at the end of every episode!

4. Why do you like this character?

I like this character because he represents the idea that "it all started with a mouse." He proves that even someone small can have a massive impact on the world and bring happiness to millions of people.
