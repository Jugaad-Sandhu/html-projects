3 Simple Rules to Stay Safe 🧠

🔑

Use Strong Passwords

Make your password long and tricky — mix letters, numbers and symbols. Never share it with anyone!

🚫

Keep Info Private

Don't tell strangers your name, address, or school online — even if they seem nice.

🗣️

Talk to a Grown-Up

If something online feels weird or scary, tell a parent or teacher straight away. You won't be in trouble!

⚠️
⚠️ Watch out for Cyberbullying!

1 in 3 kids online has been bullied. If it happens to you — tell an adult. It's never your fault.
