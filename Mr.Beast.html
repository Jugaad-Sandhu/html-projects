Mr.Beast

American YouTuber

About Him

MrBeast (Jimmy Donaldson, born May 7, 1998) is the most-subscribed individual YouTuber, known for high-budget, philanthropic, and stunt-based videos. The 27-year-old creator has amassed over 470 million subscribers, pioneered massive, viral, and fast-paced, high-production-value content, and operates companies including Feastables and MrBeast Burger.

His Motivation

MrBeast (Jimmy Donaldson) is primarily motivated by a relentless, obsession-driven desire to create the best possible videos and continuously improve, rather than just chasing money or fame. His core motivation stems from a growth mindset, finding deep fulfillment in the creative process and making a positive, philanthropic impact.

His Inspiration

MrBeast (Jimmy Donaldson) is inspired by an obsession with YouTube algorithm mechanics, a desire to create high-stakes, philanthropic entertainment, and the success of early viral creators. Key drivers include a childhood desire to support his family, overcoming Crohn’s disease, and a "go all-in" mentality to innovate content.
